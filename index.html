Training Berne, Switzerland
Jan - Apr 2017

The 3 month coding academy is followed by a 3 month trainee program. The trainee program can be extended by another 3 months. The ultimate goal is the permanent placement of trained refugees in IT-companies and IT-departments in the Canton of Berne.

Companies

There is a shortage of skilled IT-workers, especially frontend developers. We presented our idea to IT-companies of all sizes and so far the positive response has been overwhelming.
