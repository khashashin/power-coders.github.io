Impacting lives by teaching to code

Powercoders is a coding academy for refugees. The first 12 weeks boot-camp will take place from January to April 2017 in Bern.

ReBootcamp-17-BRN
Jan - Apr 2017

The 3 month coding bootcamp is followed by a 3 month trainee program. The trainee program can be extended by another 3 months. The ultimate goal of the ReBootcamp is the permanent placement of trained refugees in IT-companies and IT-departments in the Canton of Berne.
